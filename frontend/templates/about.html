  • ▸ PCA cut features from 12,288 to 100 while retaining 99.6% of variance, enabling faster SVM training with no meaningful loss of predictive signal.
  • ▸ Generalisation was strong across 20 non-overlapping test chunks — mean accuracy {{ about_info.trial_mean if about_info else "95%+" }}, with consistent performance across varied sample compositions.
